About Me

I've worked at a couple of tech startups over the past decade (as a senior exec in business development and strategy roles) and I'm currently exploring my next startup project. As a Harvard MBA and "business guy", everyone thinks I'm insane for wanting to learn how to code. That's ok, I will continue to ignore all the detractors because I know otherwise. The ability to understand technology, build tangible products, and make improvements to our solutions is incredibly rewarding...even as the business co-founder.
Outside of work, I love to surf, play guitar, sing, play soccer, travel, and eat Peruvian food! This is a sample of my work thus-far, and more cool things are on the way. Thanks for visiting my portfolio page!
